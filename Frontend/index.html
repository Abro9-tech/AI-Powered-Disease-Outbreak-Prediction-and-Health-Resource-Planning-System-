About the System

The AI Health Intelligence System is a cutting-edge platform designed to empower healthcare officials and policy makers in Kenya. By leveraging historical data and machine learning, we provide accurate forecasts for diseases like Malaria, Cholera, and Tuberculosis.

AI Predictions

Advanced machine learning models predict case counts and risk levels with high precision.

Interactive Map

Resource Planning

Get instant recommendations on hospital bed needs and medical staffing based on predicted outbreaks.

Smart Alerts

Receive immediate warnings for high-risk regions to trigger early intervention measures.
